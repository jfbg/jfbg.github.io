MySQL Lunar Event Catalog and Related Data

The lunar event catalog can be found in text format from Nakamura (1992). I converted this catalog into a mySQL databases and included epicentral locations, depths, arrival times picks for various events used in past analyses. It has proven to be pretty useful to automate our inversion work or to simply query and play with the data. I update this database occasionaly with data from articles used in my work. Feel free to download the database and see if it can be of some use to you!

Database and some explanatory materials: Here

Data comes from following articles so far:

  • Nakamura, Y. (1992), Catalog of lunar seismic data from Apollo Passive Seismic Experiment on 8-mm video cassette (exabyte) tapes, Univ. Tex. Inst. Geophys. Tech. Rep. 118, Inst. for Geophys., Univ. of Tex. at Austin, Austin.
  • Lognonné, P., J. Gagnepain-Beyneix, et H. Chenet (2003), A new seismic model of the Moon: implications for structure, thermal evolution and formation of the Moon, Earth Planet. Sci. Lett., 211(1-2), 27–44.
