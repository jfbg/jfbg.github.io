Insight 2016 Consulting Project

Staff Me Right! : Optimizing Staffing Levels at the San Diego 911 Call Center

  • Caption:

    For this consulting project I have consulted with the City of San Diego to optimize the staff levels at their 911 Call Center.

  • Caption:

    Whereas the California state standard requires 90% of the calls to be picked up in less than 10 seconds, San Diego's only achieved this for about 68% of the calls. This means that 32% of the callers had to wait for more than 10 seconds before reaching help.

  • Caption:

    That is highly problematic !

  • Caption:

    The city asked me to provide them with a predictive model of the hourly call load (how many calls per hour can the system expect). Based on this model, the city would like some recommendations on the staff number it needs to get to minimize the wait times. The city provided me with about a year worth of call data, which is over a million individual call logs

  • Caption:

    This data was cleaned up and converted to a Postgres database to facilitate data query and analysis.
